## Personalization Framework

For each prospect, I need to:
1. Visit their landing page
2. Find 1 genuine positive thing
3. Find 2-3 specific conversion issues
4. Check their competitor's pages for comparison
5. Note their pricing tier (ensures they can afford $497)

Time per prospect: ~3-5 minutes
Quality > quantity on this offer.
