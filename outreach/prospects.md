# PageRoast Prospect List

## Prospecting Strategy

### Ideal Customer Profile
- B2B SaaS founder / marketing lead
- $10K-$500K ARR (has budget, still scrappy)
- Recently launched or actively marketing
- Landing page has obvious issues
- Active on Twitter/LinkedIn

### Where to Find Them
1. **Twitter/X** - Search "just launched" + SaaS keywords
2. **Product Hunt** - Recent launches (past 30 days)
3. **Indie Hackers** - Product pages with URLs
4. **r/SaaS, r/startups** - People sharing their launches
5. **LinkedIn** - SaaS founders, filter by company size
6. **Betalist** - Startup launches
7. **HackerNews** - Show HN posts

### Qualification Checklist
Before adding to outreach list:
- [ ] Landing page has clear conversion issues
- [ ] Product appears active (not abandoned)
- [ ] Can find founder email or LinkedIn
- [ ] Not a massive company (>$10M ARR)
- [ ] B2B or prosumer (not pure consumer)
