The 5 Conversion Killers I See On Every SaaS Page

💀

Your Headline Talks About You

"We help companies..." — Nobody cares what YOU do. They care what THEY get. Your headline should hit their pain, not describe your product.

💀

Your Proof Is Buried

Testimonials at the bottom? Logos hidden? If visitors don't see proof within 5 seconds, they assume you're unproven. And they leave.

💀

Your CTA Is Invisible

"Get Started" is not a CTA. It's a shrug. A real CTA creates urgency, promises a specific outcome, and makes clicking feel like a no-brainer.

💀

No Reason To Act Now

Why should someone sign up today vs. next month? If there's no urgency, they'll "think about it" — which means they'll forget about it.

💀

Too Much Friction

Multiple CTAs. Confusing navigation. Walls of text. Every extra thing you ask visitors to think about is another chance for them to leave.
