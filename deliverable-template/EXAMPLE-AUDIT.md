## Above-The-Fold Audit 🔴 CRITICAL

### Current State:
![Screenshot of above-fold area]

### Headline Analysis

**Current:** "Welcome to [Product] — The Best Solution for Your Needs"

**Problems:**
- ❌ "Welcome to" wastes prime real estate
- ❌ "Best solution" is a claim everyone makes
- ❌ "Your needs" is vague — what needs?
- ❌ Zero specificity about outcomes

**Rewrite Options:**

**Option A (Outcome-focused):**
> "Get [Specific Result] in [Timeframe] — Without [Pain Point]"
> 
> Example: "Get 50+ 5-Star Reviews in 90 Days — Without Begging Customers"

**Option B (Pain-agitation):**
> "Tired of [Specific Pain]? There's a Better Way."
>
> Example: "Tired of Competitors Outranking You? There's a Better Way."

**Option C (Direct transformation):**
> "Go From [Before State] to [After State]"
>
> Example: "Go From Page 2 to Position #1 on Google Maps"

**Expected Impact:** +25-35% improvement in engagement  
**Priority:** 🔴 DO THIS FIRST

---

### Subheadline Analysis

**Current:** "We help businesses grow with our innovative platform."

**Problems:**
- ❌ "We help businesses grow" — every company says this
- ❌ "Innovative platform" — meaningless buzzword
- ❌ No specificity about HOW you help or WHO you help

**Rewrite:**
> "[Specific mechanism] that [specific outcome] for [specific audience]. Join [X] businesses already [achieving result]."
>
> Example: "Automated review collection that gets you to #1 on Google Maps. Join 4,000+ service businesses already dominating local search."

**Expected Impact:** +10-15% scroll depth  
**Priority:** 🟡 HIGH (do after headline)

---

### CTA Analysis

**Current:** "Get Started"

**Problems:**
- ❌ Generic — could be any product
- ❌ No value proposition in the CTA itself
- ❌ No risk-reversal or incentive

**Rewrite Options:**

| Current | Better | Best |
|---------|--------|------|
| Get Started | Start Free Trial | Start My Free 7-Day Trial |
| Sign Up | Try It Free | Get My First 10 Reviews Free |
| Learn More | See How It Works | Watch 60-Second Demo |

**A/B Test Sequence:**
1. "Start My Free Trial" vs "Get Started" (possessive language test)
2. Winner vs "Start Getting More Reviews Today" (outcome language test)
3. Winner vs "Claim Your Free Trial" (urgency language test)

**Expected Impact:** +15-25% CTA click-through rate  
**Priority:** 🟡 HIGH

## Objection Handling Audit 🟡

### Top 5 Objections (and whether you address them):

| Objection | Addressed? | Where? |
|-----------|------------|--------|
| "Is this legit or a scam?" | ❌ No | Need testimonials + trust badges |
| "What if it doesn't work for me?" | ❌ No | Need guarantee |
| "Is it hard to set up?" | Partially | Mention in FAQ, needs prominence |
| "What's the catch?" | ❌ No | Need transparent pricing |
| "Why you vs competitors?" | ❌ No | Need comparison section |

### Add This Objection-Handling Copy:

**For "Is it legit?":**
> "Trusted by 4,000+ service businesses including [3 recognizable names]. See what they're saying →"

**For "What if it doesn't work?":**
> "Try it free for 7 days. If you don't see results in 90 days, we'll refund every penny. No questions asked."

**For "Is it hard to set up?":**
> "Setup takes 5 minutes. No tech skills needed. We'll walk you through it."

**Expected Impact:** +10-20% conversion from hesitant visitors  
**Priority:** 🟡 HIGH
